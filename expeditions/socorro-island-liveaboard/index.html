Socorro Island Liveaboard

Overview

Embark on a liveaboard voyage to the remote Revillagigedo Archipelago, better known as Socorro. These volcanic islands host giant mantas, schooling sharks and other pelagic wonders rarely seen elsewhere.

Days are filled with multiple dives at legendary sites where close encounters with oceanic mantas are common. Evenings are spent swapping stories under brilliant night skies far from shore.

This expedition is suited for experienced divers seeking adventure in one of the world's most pristine underwater sanctuaries.
