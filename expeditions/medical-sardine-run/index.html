Medical Sardine Run

Overview

Follow the shimmering trails of sardines as they migrate in massive schools along the coast. Predators gather for the feast, creating one of nature's most dramatic feeding events.

You will snorkel or dive alongside the action, witnessing striped marlin, sea lions and seabirds slicing through the bait ball in synchronized strikes. Safety is our top priority, and our team positions you where the viewing is best.

This adventure moves with the fish, offering a dynamic ocean safari where each day brings new surprises.
