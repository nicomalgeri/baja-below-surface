Winter Whales

Overview

Every winter, majestic whales migrate to the warm waters of Baja to breed and give birth. Join us to observe these gentle giants as they breach, spyhop and nurture their young in protected lagoons.

Our experienced captains position the boat at respectful distances, allowing encounters that are both intimate and responsible. You may even witness curious calves approaching the surface to investigate our presence.

Beyond the whales, the surrounding desert and shoreline offer stunning vistas, making this a quintessential Baja experience.
