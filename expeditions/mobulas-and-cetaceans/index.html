Mobulas and Cetaceans

Overview

Sail out to witness the incredible ballet of mobula rays leaping from the water while whales cruise gracefully nearby. This seasonal gathering offers a front-row seat to the ocean's most acrobatic performers.

Our naturalist guides share insights into the behavior of these gentle creatures and the ecosystems that support them. With small groups on board, every sighting feels personal and unforgettable.

Keep your camera ready—dolphins, whales and mobulas often converge in spectacular displays just meters from the boat.
