Diving in the Sea of Cortez

Overview

Plunge into the turquoise waters of the Sea of Cortez and explore reefs bursting with color and life. This expedition puts you in the heart of one of the most biodiverse seas on the planet.

From playful sea lions to towering rock formations, each dive site offers a new spectacle. Our guides tailor every outing to the experience level of the group, ensuring safe and unforgettable encounters.

Between dives, relax on deck and take in desert landscapes that meet the sea. Nights anchor in tranquil coves under star-filled skies.
