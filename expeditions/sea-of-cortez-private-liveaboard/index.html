Sea of Cortez Private Liveaboard

Overview

Charter our vessel for an exclusive journey tailored entirely to your interests. Whether you dream of secluded bays, wildlife encounters or simply unplugging from the world, the Sea of Cortez delivers.

Our crew crafts a custom itinerary with flexible dive and snorkel options. Gourmet meals and comfortable cabins ensure that time on board is as memorable as time in the water.

Perfect for families or close groups seeking privacy and adventure, this liveaboard showcases Baja's hidden gems at your own pace.
