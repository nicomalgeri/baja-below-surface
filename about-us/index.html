About Us

Who We Are

At the heart of our mission lies a simple yet powerful goal: to inspire wonder and foster connection through the incredible diversity of marine life that calls the ocean home.

We believe that education is the first step toward meaningful change. By learning about the mysteries of the deep, we grow a greater appreciation for the ocean's vital role in our world and our place within it.

Our journeys are about more than exploration. They're about uncovering the unknown, encountering rare and fascinating marine species, and sharing these experiences in a way that sparks excitement and understanding.

Attention to detail and personalised service are the cornerstones of our guest experience—because no two ocean lovers are alike. Whether you're an experienced diver or embarking on your first adventure at sea, we'll design an itinerary that reflects your passions.

Above all, we are protectors of the ocean. Sustainability sits at the core of everything we do, from onboard practices to the conservation initiatives we support.

Meet Dani — Founder & Expedition Leader

Born and raised on the Mediterranean coast, Dani grew up with the sea as his playground. At 14, a first scuba dive became a life-defining moment—turning passion into purpose.

At 18, he moved to London, then to Australia to earn his scuba certifications while exploring world-class marine ecosystems.

In 2019, he settled in Baja California Sur, spending five years on liveaboard expeditions to the Socorro Islands and the Sea of Cortez.

With thousands of dives and a global network, Dani now shares the secrets of the sea—especially the Mexican waters that have captured his heart.
